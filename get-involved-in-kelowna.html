Get Involved

Looking for some ways to get more involved in emergency preparedness here in Kelowna?

Preparing

Wildfire Safety

Kelowna's at a high risk for wildfires. Learn what you need to do to minimize risk and stay safe during and after a fire.

Learn More

BC FireSmart Manual

Know what you can do to reduce the potential impacts of wildfire on your home and community.

Download PDF

Emergency Kits

Ensure that you have some basic supplies in case a disaster strikes. Learn what you should include and where to keep your kits.

Learn More

PreparedBC

The Province of BC has a range of resources, planning materials, and guides for many different types of emergencies that may affect Kelowna, including floods.

Learn More

Volunteering

BC Public Safety

Join the over 13,000 volunteers across the province in programs that assist with things like search and rescue, neighbourhood coordination, and emergency radio communications.

Learn More

Regional District Parks Programs

A range of volunteer opportunities are available in Regional Parks, including park cleanups after a storm, special events, and wildlife inventories to help measure the health of our critical ecosystems.

Learn More

Workshops and Training

Environmental Education Centre for the Okanagan

The EECO offers a variety of public programs, including 'Wild Walks', geocaching, and wellness workshops.

Learn More

Red Cross

The Red Cross has many different certification programs, training, and workshops available, such as Water Safety, First Aid, and CPR.

Learn More
