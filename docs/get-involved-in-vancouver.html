Looking for some ways to get more involved in emergency preparedness here in Vancouver?

Preparing

Earthquake Safety

Vancouver's at a high risk for a big earthquake. Learn what you need to do to stay safe during and after a quake.

PreparedBC

The Province of BC has a range of resources, planning materials, and guides for many different types of emergencies.

Ensure that you have some basic supplies in case a disaster strikes. Learn what you should include and where to keep your kits.

Disaster Support Hubs

When a disaster strikes, know where to go by printing out a map of Disaster Support Hubs located throughout Vancouver.

Volunteering

Vancouver Volunteer Corps (VVC)

The City's Volunteer Corps is designed to support emergency services by offering in-depth training programs and exercises.

Learn More

BC Public Safety

Join the over 13,000 volunteers across the province in programs that assist with things like search and rescue, neighbourhood coordination, and emergency radio communications.

Learn More

Workshops and Training

Stanley Park Ecology Society

The Ecology Society offers a variety of public programs and workshops, covering everything from birding to brewing your own bitters.

Learn More

Red Cross

The Red Cross has many different certification programs, training, and workshops available, such as Water Safety, First Aid, and CPR.
