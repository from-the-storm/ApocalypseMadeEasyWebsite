Halloween Spooptacular!

Between October 4 and October 31, 2019, the three highest-scoring teams will win Mountain Equipment Co-op gift cards! Gift cards will be mailed to the winning team's captain, to be distributed equally among teammates.

  • First Place: $250 CAD
  • Second Place: $150 CAD
  • Third Place: $100 CAD

NOTE: To qualify to win gift cards, your team must completely walk through a given scenario's route outdoors while you play.

Start surviving today! Questions? Email us info@apocalypsemadeeasy.com.

How to partcipate

  1. Gather a group of 2-5 friends or family members
  2. Pick a scenario and head to the starting point
  3. Sign up and use your generated team code to join the same team
  4. Follow the directions and see if you can complete the tasks and learn the skills necessary to survive

For more info, visit the home page.
