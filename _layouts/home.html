A free, outdoor, team survival training game that runs in your phone's web browser. Play now in Vancouver and Kelowna.

How to Play

How to Play

#1 Gather friends!

You'll need a team of 2 to 5. Once you create a team, you'll get a unique code your teammates can use to join you.

#2 Choose scenario!

Pick from four different scenarios. Then bring yourselves and your phones to its starting point.

#3 Start surviving!

Once you start, an AI named BORIS will send clues and instructions to your phones.

Scenario Map!

Choose from these four apocalyptic scenarios in Vancouver or Kelowna. For an overview of the scenarios, visit our handy FAQ section.
